As requested and for the present, the Maheswati Devi stories have been removed from the web and are no longer available here.

A second party has informed us on 8 April 2000 that a Katha publisher in New Delhi "has asked that the stories be removed from the site or that [the University of] Virginia pay a royalty for use." We are abiding by this request and are in the process of trying to contact the publishers to confirm and discuss this request.

There is a possibility these stories and others will eventually be available online, after further discussions with publishers, at another site, as part of the South Asia Digital Library project. Back to the top
