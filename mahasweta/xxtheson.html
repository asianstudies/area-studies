As requested and for the present, these wonderful stories of Maheswati Devi have been removed from the web and are no longer available here.

A second party has informed us that Geeta Dharmarajan of Katha in New Delhi "has asked that the stories be removed from the site or that [the University of] Virginia pay a royalty for use." We are in the process of trying to contact Geeta Dharmarajan to confirm this request so as to abide by the wishes of the writer, translator, or publishers.

There is a possibility these stories and others will eventually be available, after further discussions with publishers, at another site, as part of the South Asia Digital Library project.
