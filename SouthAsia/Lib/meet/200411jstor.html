JSTOR South Asia Titles

Final Lists for JSTOR suggestions for South Asian related Titles:
Memo sent out by Mary Rader, 11/15/2004

Colleagues,
Although I sent our original list of JSTOR title suggestions to JSTOR on Friday, a few more suggestions came through over the weekend. I have adjusted the lists accordingly (see below) and have re-sent the info to JSTOR. JSTOR has expressed its appreciation for these suggestions and will most likely want to follow-up with us again. Until that point, I am not taking any more suggestions.

Many thanks to all who helped with this. I do believe this is a step in the right direction for CONSALD and all those concerned with future developments for South Asian indexing and online resources.

Best,
Mary
