    MEETING
    I. Round robin
    II. Approval of Minutes http://www.lib.virginia.edu/area-studies/SouthAsia/Lib/meet/2003octdraftminutes.html
    III. Elections for four slots on the executive committee. Candidates are Rajwant Chilana (Illinois), David Magier (Columbia), David Nelson (Pennsylvania), Mary Rader (Michigan). We can discuss whether we want to change the by-laws about number of members elected every year and length of service on the executive committee. http://www.lib.virginia.edu/area-studies/SouthAsia/Lib/concon.html
    III. Bibiliography of Asian Studies (Aruna Kuruganti): status of South Asian journal indexing and analytics coverage http://ets.umdl.umich.edu/b/bas/
    IV. PAIR (Wayne Hayes). Portal to Asian Internet Resources, formerly Digital Asia Library, at U of Wisconsin has added some three thousand new annotated records to its data base since winning a second round of Title VI funding. The project has expanded Asian area coverage and is working on vernacular character display and enhanced searching. http://webcat.library.wisc.edu:3200/PAIR/
    V. Library of Congress Program Updates
