Costs and Trends of Monographic Materials
Delhi: Library of Congress
Overview of the India Program
See Tables

By Carol Mitchell, March 2000

This report is intended to give an overview of monographic materials costs and trends in the Overseas Acquisitions Program for the Library of Congress, Delhi, India, covering the period from 1996 through 1999. It examines the major categories of Indian publications in English and selected languages as well as English language programs for other South Asian countries -- Nepal, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka. In the absence of information on publishing and book production, it is impossible to place the program into the larger context of Indian publishing. For all intents and purposes, there is no information on the number, type, and price of publishing for the region. UNESCO statistics are of little use in helping assess the program because they are far from current and are of doubtful accuracy.
