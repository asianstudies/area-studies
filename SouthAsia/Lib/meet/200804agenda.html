Thursday 3 April 2008. ca 7 p.m CONSALD dinner at the Haveli Restaurant, 225 Spring St NW Atlanta, GA 30303. (404) 522-4545.  It’s about 3 blocks from the conference hotel downtown, the Hyatt Regency Atlanta.  Here’s a Google Map with directions.  Cost quoted today on the assumption we will be about 25 people is $20 per person plus tax and tip and drinks.  Dinner will be served as a mixed veg/non-veg buffet.  Reviews on this restaurant are mixed but the location offers the greatest convenience for the most people.

Transportation:

Coming from the airport...
 A. Take any train northbound to the Lindbergh MARTA station, and take the #6-Emory bus to the Emory campus.  Ask the bus driver to let you off in Emory Village near the Emory main entrance. For a bus schedule, access the MARTA homepage: http://itsmarta.com/.
B. OR Take any northbound train to Five Points station and transfer there to the Indian Creek eastbound train, get off at Decatur and take the free Emory shuttle to campus.  Disembark at TEC A (The Emory Clinic A) on Clifton Road, then cross Clifton Road and walk 1 block through the Emory Hospital dock area to the Woodruff library (See the Google map link below). 
C. OR Taxi : A cab ride from the airport to campus will cost from $30 to $40 and will take approximately 25 minutes with normal traffic.  Allow at least an hour's travel time during weekday rush hours, 7:00 a.m. until 9:00 a.m. and 3:30 p.m. until 7:00 p.m.

Coming from the Hyatt Regency
A.      MARTA: Take the southbound train at the Peachtree Center Station stop and go one stop south to the Five Points transfer station and transfer there to the Indian Creek eastbound train (not the MLK eastbound train), get off at Decatur and take the free Emory shuttle to campus.  Disembark at TEC A (The Emory Clinic A) on Clifton Road, then cross Clifton Road and walk 1 block through the Emory Hospital dock area to the Woodruff library (See the Google map link below).
B.      By car or taxi, the distance is about 4 miles.  Taxis charge $ 2.50 for the first 1/8 mile and $ 0.25 for ea. additional 1/8 mile.
