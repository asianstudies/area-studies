Azerbaijan Constitution. [NOTE: to view this Azerbaijan site, you may need to download a viewer (about 1400 kb in size) and install it; depending on your modem this might take 4 to 12 minutes.)
http://www.president.az/azerbaijan/const.htm
Prepared by the Commission for draft preparation of new Constitution of the Azerbaijan Republic under chairmanship of President of the Azerbaijan Republic Heydar Aliyev, adopted on 12 of November 1995 by the Referendum of the Azerbaijan Republic. Came into force since 27 of November 1995.
