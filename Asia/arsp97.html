Asia Video Resources on the World Wide Web
by Raymond Lum - Spring 1997 (Print source*)

In response to a request to do a thematic version of this column, I have chosen Asian video resources as the focus here. This is part of a larger database, in process, that I am preparing for the M-ASIA web page (http://h-net.msu.edu/~asia/). Please send information on additional resources to:

rlum@fas.harvard.edu

Those AAS members who have sent me infomnation on their web pages can be assured that those will appear in future columns.

http://www.virginia.edu/~soasia/videos/videolist.html
Previous gopher site - gopher://minerva.acc.Virginia.edu:70/00/pubs/soasia/filmsall.csa
Films and videos on South Asia available for free loan to educators in the Southeastern U.S. [Also this Indian Video Collection is searchable.]

gopher://gopher.adp.wisc.edu:70/11,.browse/.METALIBSE, updated to
http://www.library.wisc.edu/databases/SEAvideo/
List of videos on South and Southeast Asia available on interlibrary loan; searchable by keywordl inks to indices by country, language, videos with English sub-titles.

gopher://gopher.cc.columbia.edu:71/11/clioplus/scholarly/SouthAsia/Bibinfo/VID
Links to South Asia video archives at University of Wisconsin, University of Virgina, University of Califomia, Berkeley; documentary films and videos on South Asian religions; English-language films on India and Indians (a filmography); sources for purchases.

[Working ?] http://webhead.com/wwwvl/India/india213.html
[Try -] http://webhead.com/wwwvl/India/india2.html And click on Movies
A large number of links to information in Indian movies; part of the WWW Virtual Library India. Includes information on the films of Satyajit Ray, Urdu/Hindi film music, ragas commonly used in Indian films, and much on Bollywood.

http://www.lib.berkeley.edu/MRC/SSEAsiaMenu.html
Information on videos about South and Southeast Asia; links to films by South and Southeast Asian film makers; Asian American videos: race and ethnicity; overseas South Asian communities; documentaries; Vietnam War.

http://www.cee.umn.edu/ufv/
Includes Asians/Asian Americans mediography; alphabetical list, with full information including rental information from Univ. of Minnesota's film and video center.

[Working ?] http://garnet.berkeley.edu:4252/acad.html#Teaching
[Try -] http://www.lib.berkeley.edu/MRC/SSEAsiaVid.html
South Asia, Southeast Asia, Oceania videotapes available on the Berkeley campus.

http://www.jyu.fi/~tjko/hkmovie
A Hong Kong movies searchable database; filmographies, interviews, reviews.

http://egret0.stanford.edu/hk/about.html
On Hong Kong movies, with databases searchable by titles, names of people associated with films; capsule reviews; where to buy films; news; awards; numerous links, including Documentary Box, a joumal on recent trends in thinking about and making documentaries.

http://pears.lib.ohio-state.edu/Markus/Filmbib.html
Bibliography of Japanese films.

http://www.lib.berkeley.edu/MRC/CJKVid.html
China, Japan, Korea, Pacific Islands videos in the Media Resource Center, Moffitt Library, Univ. of California. Berkeley; gives synopses, length, citations for reviews.

http://www.naatanet.org/
Created to address the scarcity of Asian images in films, to correct distorted images of Asians in mainstream media, to create opportunities for Asian Amencan film makers; source of many of the PBS films on Asian Americans; covers all of Asia; for all age groups; includes ethnic heritage, Japanese intemment, aging, activism, art, and performance.

http://www.tufts.edu/%7Estai/QAPA/films.html
Gay and lesbian Asian and Pacific films; long annotated list; list of distributors; link to article on Asian/Pacific gay and lesbian film makers.

http://www.cs.earlham.edu/~cem
Searchable database of Earlham College's Center for Education media materials, including videos, audio cassettes, CD-ROMs, curriculum units, slides, videos; searchable by country/culture, media type, audience, title, date, keyword, discipline/subject; annotated list of K-12 and research-level materials on Asia; for videos, provides price, distributors; links to other Centers.

http://asnic.utexas.edu/asnic/outreach/layout.html
This and other Asia-specific National Resource Centers include outreach programs for educators and the community. This one has list of videos on Asia that can be borrowed, along with books, slides, curriculum materials. [See Newest Resources]

http://www.filmakers.com
Homepage of Filmakers Library, a commercial firm; rentals and sales; numerous videos on Asia under headings "Asia," "Multi-Culturalism."

[Works?] http://www.cibtc.co.cn/
Homepage of China International Book Trading Corporation, Beijing; includes list of videos for sale.

Films on Korea are available from The Korean Cultural Service, 460 Park Ave., 6th floor, New York, NY 10022; Tel. (212) 759-9550; FAX (212) 688-8640.

A catalog of films and videos on Asian and Asian American Studies is available from University of California Extension Center for Media and Independent Learning, 2000 Center St., 4th floor, Berkeley, CA 94704; sales and rentals; Tel. (510) 642-0460; FAX (510) 643-9271.

Films for the Humanities and Sciences issues a catalog of Asian studies video programs: P.O. Box 2053, Princeton, NJ 08543-2053; sales and rentals; Tel. (800) 257-5126.

Facets Video, 1517 W. Fullerton, Chicago 60614, issues a video sales catalog several times each year that includes Asia-related titles. A cumulative catalog of Asian titles is issued irregularly.
